## **Risk Lists**
1. Incorrect price displayed for selected parcel size or destination.
2. Customer is charged multiple times (double click, retry, refresh)
3. Payment succeeds but order is not created
4. Cart shows stale data after edits or page refresh.
5. Order created but payment not captured
6. Cart items or totals do not persist across devices/sessions.
7. Payment fails mid-transaction or is double-charged.
8. Invalid, expired or incorrect discount codes applied incorrectly.
9. Payment gateway or transaction errors not handled correctly.
10. Carrier API downtime causes failed label generation.
11. Payment succeeds but shipping label is not generated
12. Wrong label generated (wrong address, weight, service)
13. Tracking number missing or invalid
14. Label generated multiple times for one order
15. User cannot recover order after refresh or navigation away
16. Payment gateway timeouts(3ds or master)
17. Wrong parcel size or address sent to carrier causing delivery failure.
18. Customs issues result in delayed delivery or unexpected fees.
19. Layout breaks on different devices or screen orientations.
20. Slow network causes duplicate “Add to Cart” or timeout errors.
21. Backend failures (pricing, cart, carrier APIs) not communicated to the user.
22. Sessions expire or reset mid-flow resulting in lost orders.
23. Failed shipments or delayed deliveries cause refunds and operational overhead.
